Best-Value Restaurants in Berlin



There are so many good restaurants in Berlin , but it is sometimes hard to find

both cheap and good places. It turns out that the Yelp database might help.

I looked at 5,000 restaurants in Berlin and would like to share the results.



Dark green = Highest value restaurants (5 stars rating and Under EUR 10))
